My Vintage Clothing Hobby

I've been collecting vintage clothing since 2016. My favorite thing about vintage clothing is the amount of information I'm able to learn from it, specifically band/concert t-shirts. For most of my life I was influenced by modern hip hop and pop music. But through vintage clothing, I'm able to learn about different types of music during all time periods. I feel as with how technology is advancing and moving forward so fast, it's nice sometimes to step back and see what we had in the past.
